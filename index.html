Challenge

Launch a brand-new B2B SaaS platform from scratch to give grocery retailers e-commerce capabilities. The hardest part was defining MVP scope—balancing ambitious vision with tight timelines while building something retailers would actually use.

Dott Digital Wallet

B2C e-commerce · Retention & Monetization

2020–2022

Challenge

Strong acquisition but weak repeat purchases. Lack of loyalty mechanisms limited customer lifetime value. The goal: drive retention without adding checkout complexity, using circular cashback to incentivize behavior.

Approach

Built a digital wallet with a twist—incentives that prompt shoppers to repurchase or lose accrued cashback. Partnered with Marketing and Data Science to model incentive structures and cashback tiers by segment, testing to balance user value and unit economics.

Solution

Launched a wallet with tiered cashback scaled to spend, encouraging more frequent purchases. Balance became a natural retention hook, paired with seamless payment integration and smart checkout prompts.
