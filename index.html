GlovoXL 0-to-1 Launch

B2B SaaS Platform • 6-Month MVP Journey

2023-2025

🎯 Challenge

Tasked with launching a brand-new B2B SaaS platform from scratch to empower grocery retailers with e-commerce capabilities. The biggest challenge was defining the MVP scope — balancing ambitious vision with tight delivery timelines while ensuring we built something retailers would actually use.

🔍 Approach

I led extensive discovery work watching competitors like instacart. Rather than building a full-featured platform, I ruthlessly prioritized features based on three criteria: retailer value, technical feasibility, and competitive differentiation. I worked closely with engineering to define a microservices architecture that would allow us to scale post-launch with more flexibility built-in in core concepts.

✅ Solution

Defined and delivered an MVP focused on the core order management flow: dual mobile apps (picker apps), order orchestration services, and essential retailer apis/webhooks. Established clear product principles and technical requirements that guided the team through complex trade-off decisions. Built comprehensive E2E testing from day one to ensure quality at speed.

Dott Digital Wallet Feature

B2C eCommerce Feature • Retention & Monetization

2020-2022

🎯 Challenge

Our e-commerce marketplace had strong customer acquisition but struggled with repeat purchases. Building purchase habits and lack of loyalty mechanisms were limiting customer lifetime value. The challenge was to drive meaningful retention improvements without adding complexity to the shopping experience, while applying circular cashbacks without too much complexity to incentivize behaviour from the customer.

🔍 Approach

I delivered a digital wallet feature with a strategic twist: added incentives to make shoppers repruchase or loose their cashback from previous purchases. I collaborated with Marketing and Data Science to model different incentive structures, different customer segments with different cashback tiers, running tests to optimize the balance between user value and unit economics. Integrated 3 new payment methods to reduce checkout friction and expand accessibility.

✅ Solution

Launched a digital wallet with tiered incentives: customers who purchased frequently were given cashback in tiers depending on the amount spent, encouraging purchasing more often. The wallet became a natural retention tool—users with balance were more likely to return for their next purchase. Combined this with seamless payment integration and smart prompts during the checkout flow.
